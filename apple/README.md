iPhone
iPhone 8, X, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16 (Face ID & Touch ID SE 2/3)
Recovery Mode:

Connect to computer.

Press & release Volume Up.

Press & release Volume Down.

Press & hold Side Button until the "Computer + Cable" icon appears (do not release at Apple Logo).
​

DFU Mode (Black Screen):

Connect to computer.

Press & release Volume Up.

Press & release Volume Down.

Press & hold Side Button for 10 seconds (Screen goes black).

While holding Side, press & hold Volume Down for 5 seconds.

Release Side Button but keep holding Volume Down until the computer detects a device in recovery (Device screen stays black).
​

iPhone 7 & 7 Plus
Recovery Mode:

Connect to computer.

Press & hold Side Button + Volume Down together.

Keep holding until the Recovery Mode screen appears.
​

DFU Mode:

Connect to computer.

Press & hold Side Button + Volume Down for 8 seconds.

Release Side Button but keep holding Volume Down until detected by computer.
​

iPhone 6s, SE (1st Gen) & Earlier
Recovery Mode:

Connect to computer.

Press & hold Home Button + Top (or Side) Power Button together.

Keep holding until the Recovery Mode screen appears.
​

DFU Mode:

Connect to computer.

Press & hold Home Button + Top (or Side) Power Button for 8 seconds.

Release Power Button but keep holding Home Button until detected by computer.
​

iPad
iPad with Face ID (No Home Button)
Recovery Mode:

Press & release Volume Up.

Press & release Volume Down.

Press & hold Top Power Button until Recovery screen appears.
​

DFU Mode:

Press & release Volume Up.

Press & release Volume Down.

Press & hold Top Power Button for 10 seconds.

While holding Power, press & hold Volume Down for 5 seconds.

Release Power, hold Volume Down until detected.
​

iPad with Home Button
Recovery Mode:

Press & hold Home Button + Top Power Button together until Recovery screen appears.
​

DFU Mode:

Press & hold Home Button + Top Power Button for 8 seconds.

Release Power, keep holding Home until detected.
​

Mac (macOS)
Apple Silicon (M1, M2, M3, M4)
Startup Options (Recovery):

Shut down completely.

Press & hold Power Button until "Loading startup options" appears. Select Options > Continue.
​

DFU Mode (For Apple Configurator / Revive):

Connect USB-C cable to the Back Left port (MacBook) or specific DFU port (Desktop).

Shut down.

Press & hold Power Button.

At the same time, press & hold: Left Control + Left Option + Right Shift.

Hold all 4 keys for 10 seconds.

Release the keyboard keys but keep holding Power for another 5+ seconds until detected in Apple Configurator 2.
​
​

Intel Macs with T2 Security Chip (2018-2020)
Recovery Mode: Cmd (⌘) + R during boot.
​

Internet Recovery: Cmd (⌘) + Option (⌥) + R (Latest OS) OR Shift + Cmd + Option + R (Original OS).

DFU Mode (For Apple Configurator):

Connect USB-C to the Front Left port (MacBook) or specific DFU port.

Shut down.

Press Power Button for 1 second.

Immediately press & hold: Right Shift + Left Option + Left Control.

Hold all 4 keys (including Power) for 10 seconds.

Release all keys (Device remains black, check Configurator).
​

Intel Macs (Non-T2)
Recovery Mode: Cmd (⌘) + R.

Boot Picker: Hold Option (⌥) during boot.

Reset NVRAM/PRAM: Cmd + Option + P + R (Hold for 2 chimes).

Safe Mode: Hold Shift during boot.
​

Apple Watch & Apple TV
Apple Watch
Wireless Firmware Restore (Series 3+, watchOS 8.5+):

If watch shows red "!" icon: Double-click Side Button.

Bring unlocked iPhone (iOS 15.4+) nearby.

Follow prompts on iPhone.
​
​

Apple TV
Apple TV 4K (No USB Port):

Wireless Recovery (tvOS 17+): Bring unlocked iPhone close to Apple TV if black screen/issue persists (Automatic prompt).

Force Recovery Menu: Unplug power → Plug in → Unplug immediately when light turns on. Repeat 5-6 times in rapid succession.
​

Apple TV HD / Earlier (USB-C or Micro-USB):

Unplug HDMI & Power.

Connect via USB to Computer (iTunes/Finder).

Plug in Power. Hold Menu + Play/Pause for 6 seconds (if not detected automatically).
